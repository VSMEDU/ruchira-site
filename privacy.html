1. Who we are

Ruchira Indian Cuisine is a restaurant located at 2650 King Road, Suite 400, Frisco, TX 75036. This policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit our website at ruchiraindiancuisine.com.

2. Information we collect

We do not collect personal information directly on this website. However, the following third-party services may collect data when you visit:

  • Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics — collects anonymized usage data (pages visited, session duration, device type) via cookies. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Toast Tab — when you place an order through Toast's hosted ordering page, Toast collects your name, payment information, and order details. See Toast's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address.

3. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • _ga, _gid — Google Analytics cookies used to distinguish users and sessions. These expire after 2 years and 24 hours respectively.
  • site_pin_ok — a session cookie used to authenticate access to the site. It contains no personal data.
  • cookie_consent — stores your cookie preference. Expires after 1 year.

You can opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. How we use information

Analytics data is used solely to understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it. We do not sell or share this data with third parties beyond the services listed above.

5. Your rights (Texas TDPSA)

To exercise these rights, email us at order.ruchirafrisco@gmail.com.
